A conversation with Madison Williams... or something like that

These are Camila's questions

These are Madison's answers

Why do you want to design?

I like creating things out of nothing and getting to explore my imagination for new and exciting ideas.

What is difficult or frustrating for you within design and how do you work to push past that?

What I find frustrating is being stretched too thin between projects and classes that we aren’t able to be as creative and explorative as we want because were more focused on deadlines and not falling behind

What do you see yourself doing after you finish this program?

I hope to see myself working for a company where I’m ablate have the freedom of creating things I’m passionate about.

What excites you about design?

The idea that you can put something out into to word and each person who may come across it and perceive it different and feel a way towards it you could never of imagined.

What worries you about design?

I think the same thing that excited me also worries me because something you love and worked hard on could be interpreted in a negative way.

Can design change the world?

Design already is changing the world, everything you see and consume has elements of design. Design impacts individuals different and you have no idea what stems from those feelings. ex. A poster created for presidency could go on to be the reason someone votes.

What is a website?

A website is a form of design located on the web that has the power to express an endless source of information.
